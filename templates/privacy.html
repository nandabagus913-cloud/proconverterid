Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At ProConverter, accessible from proconverterid.site, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ProConverter and how we use it.

1. Data Minimization & File Security

As a utility service, we strictly follow a "Zero Retention" policy to ensure your documents never fall into the wrong hands:

  • No Storage: Your uploaded files are processed in-memory and are automatically deleted immediately after conversion or after 1 hour of inactivity.
  • No Human Access: No staff member can access or view your uploaded documents. The process is fully automated.
  • Encryption: All file transfers between your browser and our server are protected via SSL/TLS encryption.

2. Log Files

ProConverter follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Advertising Partners

Some of advertisers on our site may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners include Google AdSense. Each of our advertising partners has their own Privacy Policy for their policies on user data.

5. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. ProConverter does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.

6. Your Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
